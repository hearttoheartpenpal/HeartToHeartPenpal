About

Heart-to-Heart is a student-led pen-pal program that connects Dunlap High School students with seniors in local nursing homes through thoughtful letters. The goal is simple: brighten someone’s day and build meaningful connections across generations.

Through this program students and seniors can share stories, encouragement, and everyday moments while building friendships rooted in kindness and community.

  • Students and seniors are paired as pen pals 💌
  • Letters are exchanged throughout the semester ✉️
  • Conversations focus on hobbies, memories, and positive stories 🌷
  • A small letter can make someone feel seen and valued ❤️

Rules

Heart-to-Heart is meant to be a safe, uplifting, and respectful program for both students and seniors. Every letter should reflect kindness, encouragement, and care.

  • 💗 Be respectful, kind, and age-appropriate
  • Do not share personal contact information (no HIPAA violations!), addresses, or schedules
  • Keep topics positive: hobbies, memories, school life, and encouragement
  • No inappropriate, romantic, political, or sensitive personal content
